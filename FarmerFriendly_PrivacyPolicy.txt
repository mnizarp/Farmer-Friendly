Thank you for choosing FarmerFriendly! This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services, including any chat facilities.

Information We Collect:
a. Personal Information: When you register on FarmerFriendly, we may collect your name, address, phone number, email address, and other relevant information.
b. Location Information: We use Google Maps for scheduling pickups. The app may collect and process your location data to facilitate the pickup scheduling and delivery process.
c. Chat Information: When you use our chat facility, we may collect and store your chat history, including messages and any shared media.

How We Use Your Information:
a. Schedule Pickups: We use your personal information to schedule and coordinate vegetable pickups from farmers.
b. Communication: We may use your contact information to emergency inquiries regarding the sales pickups.
c. Chat Support: Information shared in the chat facility is used to provide customer support, address inquiries, and improve our services.

Sharing of Information:
a. Farmers and Wholesalers: Your basic information may be shared with farmers and wholesalers involved in the pickup and delivery process.
b. Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers, such as mapping services, to facilitate our services.
c. Chat Support Agents: Information shared in the chat facility may be accessible to our customer support agents for the purpose of assisting you.

Data Security:
a. We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.
b. While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Choices:
a. You can update your account information at any time through the app.
b. You may opt-out of receiving promotional communications from us by using the unsubscribe link in the emails we send.
c. You can request the deletion of your chat history by contacting our support team.

Children's Privacy:
a. FarmerFriendly is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to the Privacy Policy:
a. We reserve the right to update or change our Privacy Policy at any time. The updated policy will be effective immediately upon posting.

By using FarmerFriendly, you agree to the terms and conditions outlined in this Privacy Policy.
